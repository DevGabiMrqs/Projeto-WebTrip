FUN FACTS

Italy's flag represents hope, faith and charity. Green is hope, white denotes faith and red is the colour of charity.
Toss a coin into the Trevi and you'll return to Rome. That's the legend. Tourists toss around €3,000 a day into the fountain. This amounts to roughly €1,000,000 every year, which is all donated to charity.
Italy has one of Europe's longest histories, yet it's only been a country since 1861. In Roman times Italy was a single entity. It then divided into a collection of sovereign states and remained that way until 1861.
